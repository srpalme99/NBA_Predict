01
Rolling stats
Every team's last 10 games are averaged across points, FG%, rebounds, assists, and turnovers. Current form beats season-long averages.
02
Elo ratings
Two parallel systems — full-season and 25-game rolling — are blended 50/50. Captures long-term quality and recent momentum in one number.
03
Player Impact Score
Players are scored using BPM, VORP, and WS/48 from Basketball Reference, weighted and normalized to a single PIS per player.
04
Injury adjustment
Injuries are scraped live from ESPN. Stars (top-3 PIS) are fully deducted; role players are partially deducted based on their impact weight.
05
Calibration
Platt scaling converts raw model scores into calibrated probabilities. A 70% prediction should win around 70% of the time.
06
Bias correction
Training is balanced 50/50 so the model can't cheat by always picking home teams. The test set uses the real 59% home-win distribution.
