What we do
Every edge,
quantified.

CourtIQ ingests live NBA data daily to surface edges most people miss — from injury-adjusted rosters to Elo drift.

Real-time injury adjustments
Player Impact Scores are recalculated the moment ESPN updates the injury report — late scratches priced in before tip-off.
Gradient boosted spreads
A GBR margin model predicts point differential. Win probability flows from norm.cdf — not a raw classifier guess.
Meta-stacker ensemble
A second-layer meta model blends the spread prediction with the base classifier for tighter, more calibrated outputs.
Live advanced stats
E_NET_RATING, E_PACE, TS%, E_AST_RATIO — fetched from the NBA API each day and diffed HOME − AWAY at inference.
Elo with a reality check
Elo ratings blended toward win-pct-implied ratings to correct for hot streaks the base Elo lags on.
Schedule & fatigue factors
Back-to-backs, road trip length, games in last 7 days, and EWMA-weighted recent form all baked into the feature vector.
